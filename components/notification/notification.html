Notification Paragraph

Notification title

This paragraph block replaces the standard subtitle. It can be a multiline

paragraph, or even include more complex elements, like:

  • Unordoned lists
  • Ordoned lists
  • Very long lines of text with specific formating

    </svg>

      <p class="sdt-inline-notification__title">Notification title</p>
      <div class="sdt-inline-notification__paragraph">
        <p>
          This paragraph block replaces the standard subtitle. It can be a multiline
        </p>
        <p>
          paragraph, or even include more complex elements, like:
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li>Unordoned lists</li>
